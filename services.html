Our Medical Services

Najali Care provides a wide range of advanced healthcare services tailored to meet your needs. Our services include expert consultations, surgeries, wellness programs, and chronic disease management.

Oncology

Comprehensive cancer care services, including diagnosis, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and post-treatment care. Our team of oncologists provides personalized care and support throughout the treatment journey.

Reproductive Health

We offer reproductive health services that focus on fertility treatments, prenatal care, gynecological surgeries, and maternal health, ensuring both mother and baby receive the highest level of care.

Geriatric Medicine

Specialized care for elderly patients, addressing complex age-related medical conditions such as dementia, arthritis, and diabetes. We focus on improving quality of life and managing chronic conditions effectively.
